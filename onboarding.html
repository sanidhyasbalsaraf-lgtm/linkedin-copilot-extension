LinkedIn Copilot
Welcome

Draft LinkedIn outreach in your own voice.

This extension reads a job posting's description and hiring-team info, then drafts a connection note, InMail, or reply — in your writing style, not a generic template. You review and send everything yourself.

  • Capture — grab the job description and hiring-manager info from any LinkedIn posting.
  • Draft — generate a message using your background and past writing samples.
  • Send — insert it straight into LinkedIn's message box, or copy it yourself.

Used to sign off drafted messages where a sign-off makes sense.

Connect

Add your OpenAI API key.

Drafts are generated by calling OpenAI directly from your browser. Your key is stored only in this browser's local storage and is used solely to call api.openai.com — never sent anywhere else.

Don't have a key yet? Get one at platform.openai.com/api-keys — you can skip this step and add it later from Settings.

Your voice

Teach it how you write.

The more real writing you give it, the less it sounds like AI. Paste a couple of past messages and a line about your background — you can always add more in Settings later.

Appearance

Pick a look, and you're set.

You can change this anytime in Settings.

Look for the "LC" button in the bottom-right corner of any LinkedIn page — that's the full panel. There's also a quick capture button right above every job's "About the job" section.
